PERKS

Juggernog is a vital perk in Call of Duty Zombies that increases the player's health, allowing them to withstand more damage from zombie attacks. This perk is essential for surviving higher rounds and tougher zombie encounters.

Speed Cola is a perk in Call of Duty Zombies that reduces the time it takes to reload weapons, enabling players to maintain a higher rate of fire and minimize vulnerability during reloads. This perk is crucial for high-intensity situations and heavy weaponry.

Stamin-Up is a perk in Call of Duty Zombies that increases the player's sprint duration and speed, allowing for quicker navigation and evasion of zombie hordes. It's especially useful for maps with large areas and objectives that require fast movement.

Double Tap Root Beer is a perk in Call of Duty Zombies that increases the player's rate of fire and doubles the bullets fired per shot, effectively boosting damage output. This perk is essential for quickly eliminating zombies and managing large waves more efficiently.
