MAPS

Origins

Origins is a map in Call of Duty: Black Ops II. It features the four original characters in a dieselpunk World War I setting. The map is known for its complex Easter egg, unique elemental staffs, and the introduction of the Panzer Soldat.

Moon

Moon is a map in Call of Duty: Black Ops. Set on a lunar base, it features low gravity, a challenging environment with no breathable atmosphere, and the notorious Astronaut zombie. The map includes a dramatic Easter egg that triggers a massive in-game event.

Der Eisendrache

Der Eisendrache is a map in Call of Duty: Black Ops III. Set in an Austrian castle, it features a medieval theme with dragon statues, the Wrath of the Ancients bow, and an intricate Easter egg involving the summoning of the Keeper. The map is highly regarded for its engaging gameplay and storyline.
