Origins

Origins immerses players in a thrilling dieselpunk World War I setting with its iconic characters. This map is renowned for its intricate Easter egg quests, where players wield elemental staffs and face the formidable Panzer Soldat.

Moon

Moon challenges players on a lunar base with low gravity and a hostile atmosphere. Navigate through this unique environment while encountering the infamous Astronaut zombie and triggering spectacular in-game events through a dramatic Easter egg.

Der Eisendrache

Der Eisendrache transports players to a medieval Austrian castle, featuring majestic dragon statues and the powerful Wrath of the Ancients bow. Explore this map's rich narrative as you uncover secrets, summon the Keeper, and engage in thrilling battles. These descriptions aim to capture the excitement and thematic elements of each map, enhancing the overall appeal and engagement for players.
